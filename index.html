About Me

I am pursuing a Master of Engineering under the Systems and Computer Engineering Department at Carleton University, specializing in the Technology Innovation Management (TIM) Program. I bring around 10 years of diverse professional experience across SaaS, Agent AI, fashion technology, engineered sportswear, and global supply chain operations.

As a business professional and entrepreneur, I have founded and scaled ventures like EasyFlex Activewear and co-developed Versali — bridging AI innovation and fashion design to create affordable, high-performance activewear for global markets.

I am also a TIM Ambassador and recipient of both the Nicol Business Idea Award 2024 and the Nicol Business Internship Award for excellence in innovation and entrepreneurship at Carleton University.

Research & Achievements

  • Perceiving Fashion through AI: CUREB-approved study exploring how large language models can generate human-aligned fashion element descriptions from product images.
  • AI Agent Development: Working on conversational agents and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems for decision-making and supply-chain automation.
  • Entrepreneurship: Founder of EasyFlex Activewear under Loomor, focused on sustainable and tech-integrated production between Bangladesh and Canada.
  • Innovation Leadership: Serving as TIM Ambassador and Professional Representative at the Carleton Engineering Graduate Students’ Association (CEGSA).

Experience Highlights

  • Actio Digital Canada Inc. — Business Development Team, contributing to Actio AI projects in process automation and intelligent OKR management.
  • ZASA Limited (UK): Assistant Manager of Merchandising — led multi-million-dollar projects with H&M, Puma, Oberalp, and Helly Hansen.
  • Crystal Group & Fakir Apparels: Research and Development roles in performance textiles, engineered sportswear, and manufacturing process optimization.
  • Versali / EasyFlex Activewear: Co-founder and strategist for next-gen AI-integrated apparel solutions.

Current Focus

I am exploring how Agent AI and retrieval-augmented intelligence can optimize operations across industries, from SaaS platforms to apparel ecosystems. My work merges engineering precision with creative entrepreneurship — developing scalable, ethical, and tech-driven solutions for the global market.
