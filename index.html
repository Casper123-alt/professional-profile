About Me

I am a Technology Innovation Management (TIM) master's student at Carleton University and serve as a TIM ambassador. With over eight years of experience across supply chain management, research & development and business development, I have worked in the UK, Hong Kong, the USA and Canada. My entrepreneurial journey includes founding EasyFlex Activewear, a brand dedicated to accessible and comfortable clothing. Currently, I work in SaaS consulting and explore large language models, retrieval‑augmented generation and intelligent agents.

I recently received the 2024 Nicol Internship Award and represent graduate students on Carleton’s Graduate Students’ Association council. I'm passionate about leveraging AI to drive innovation in business and the fashion industry.

Research & Interests

  • AI & Fashion: Exploring how artificial intelligence can perceive and understand fashion, enabling personalized styling and sustainable design.
  • Perceiving Fashion through AI: Investigating whether large language models can generate human‑aligned descriptions of fashion elements from product images, helping build human‑aligned conversational fashion recommender systems.
  • SaaS & Business Development: Driving business growth through software‑as‑a‑service solutions and strategic partnerships.
  • Supply Chain & R&D: Applying engineering principles and data analytics to optimize supply chains and product development.
  • AI Agents & RAG: Studying large language models, retrieval‑augmented generation and intelligent agents for next‑generation applications.
  • Entrepreneurship: Building startups that bridge technology and human needs, such as EasyFlex Activewear.

Current Focus

My current work centres on integrating AI into the fashion industry and SaaS consulting. Through my research on “Perceiving Fashion through AI,” I study how machine learning models can recognize patterns, textures and styles to deliver personalized recommendations and enhance customer experiences. I’m also experimenting with retrieval‑augmented generation to build intelligent agents that streamline workflows and support decision‑making.
